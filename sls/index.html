Copy and paste letter to send to your GP about the SLS changes in the NHS drug tarrif!
Applies to Buserelin, Gonadorelin, Goserelin, Leuprorelin, Nafarelin and Triptorelin.

Remember to change
- your doctor's name
- your antiandrogen's name
- your name

Message for Dr (Dr’s name):

Please note that due to the recent "Selected List Scheme" change to the drugs tariff, from now on, my (antiandrogen name) prescription will need to have the letters “SLS” on them. For more information please see https://faq.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/knowledgebase/article/KA-03227/en-us and page 406 of the drug tariff: https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/pharmacies-gp-practices-and-appliance-contractors/drug-tariff
